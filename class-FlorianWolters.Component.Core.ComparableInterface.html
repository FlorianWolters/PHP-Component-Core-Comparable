Interface ComparableInterface

The interface FlorianWolters\Component\Core\ComparableInterface imposes a total ordering on the objects of each class that implements it.

This ordering is referred to as the class's natural ordering, and the class's FlorianWolters\Component\Core\ComparableInterface::compareTo() method is referred to as its natural comparison method.
